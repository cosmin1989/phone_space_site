1. What the app does

SpaceBack helps you review, remove and compress files stored on your Android device: photos, videos, screenshots, downloads, APK/XAPK installers, junk files and unused apps. All analysis happens on your device.

You are the cleaner — we're the broom. The app never removes anything you didn't explicitly select, and cleaning is trash-first: photos and videos go to Android's own system trash (restorable for approximately 30 days, managed by Android), and other files go to SpaceBack's built-in trash bin with a configurable restore window. The one exception is APK/XAPK installer files, which are deleted permanently — the button says so before you tap it. Still, you are responsible for what you choose to remove. Please review before you confirm — especially before emptying trash, deleting installers, or deleting originals after compression.
