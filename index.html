Swipe-to-clean sessions

Review your photo library month by month — swipe right to keep, left to trash, tap to undo. Finish a month, cross it off. On This Day resurfaces old memories while you tidy.

Photo & video compression

On-device re-encoding (HEVC for video) typically recovers 50–80% of media size. The compressed copy is saved before the original ever moves to trash.

Duplicates & similar photos

Exact copies found by file analysis; similar and blurry shots by on-device perceptual scanning. Keep-best preselected — you stay in control.

The biggest files first, old downloads, leftover APKs — plus apps ranked by last use and size, uninstallable in one tap.

Everything you clean goes to the Android system trash with a ~30-day restore window. One batched confirmation — never a dialog per photo.
